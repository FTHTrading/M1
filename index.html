When an institution moves capital via bank wire, that is M1 money in motion. Our platform sits exactly at that conversion point — receiving M1 fiat, enforcing full institutional controls, and issuing programmable on-chain value backed 1:1 by held reserves.

How It Works

The 8-Stage
Settlement Lifecycle

From wire receipt to settled on-chain position — every stage of the M1 monetary workflow, governed by institutional-grade controls and posted to a GAAP-compliant double-entry ledger in real time.

1
Client Onboarding
Entity verified through AML/KYC. Wallets and bank accounts linked. Compliance profile created for the institution.
2
Request Submitted
Client initiates a mint or redemption via the dashboard or API. The request is logged, referenced, and held pending compliance review.
3
Compliance Gate
Automated policy evaluation runs in under 100 ms. Flagged transactions are held for human review. Cleared transactions advance automatically.
4
Approval Flow
Multi-signature approval by authorized operators. No single point of authority — every large transaction requires quorum sign-off.
5
Funds Confirmed
Wire reference matched to the request. Bank funding confirmed. The settlement engine picks up the job from the queue automatically.
6
Chain Settlement
Stablecoins minted on the target chain via Circle or OTC provider. Transaction anchored on Apostle Chain. Client wallet credited. Confirmation returned to the settlement engine in under 3 seconds.
7
Ledger Posting
Automated GAAP journal entry posted across the 12-account Chart of Accounts. Fiat cash debited, stablecoin inventory credited, client obligations settled. Every debit matches every credit.
8
3-Way Reconciliation
Ledger balances reconciled against bank statement and provider data. Breaks above tolerance threshold trigger automated alerts. Full reconciliation report generated and exportable for audit.
